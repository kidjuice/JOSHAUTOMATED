FAQ

Common Questions

What is included with every bot?
Every bot includes the compiled MT4 and MT5 EA files, a commercial license, a full user manual, and direct activation support. Higher tiers include additional modules and dedicated onboarding.
Does it work with funded accounts (FTMO, The5%ers)?
Yes. JOSH STB™ includes a Funded Account Mode built for prop firm risk parameters. Daily loss limits, max drawdown caps, and weekend close rules are configurable. Discuss your challenge rules with us before deploying.
Which assets does it trade?
JOSH STB™ supports all major MT4/MT5 instruments — Forex (EURUSD, GBPUSD, USDJPY, etc.), Indices (NAS100, SP500, US30), Commodities, Crypto CFDs, and Stock CFDs. Note: XAUUSD is restricted for retail US clients under CFTC regulations.
Do I need a VPS?
Strongly recommended for uninterrupted 24/5 operation. MT4/MT5 must stay running with a stable connection. We advise on VPS setup during onboarding. Higher tiers include dedicated VPS setup support.
What are the risks and drawdowns?
Historical max drawdown is 8–12% depending on tier and settings. Drawdown protection can halt trading if a threshold is breached. All trading carries risk. Past performance does not guarantee future results. We recommend starting on demo before going live.
How do I receive the bot after payment?
After payment you receive an automatic confirmation email with your download link, license document, and user manual. Our team follows up via Telegram to assist with activation and setup.
